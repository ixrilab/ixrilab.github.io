Logo Concept — I · XR · I

The IXRI mark consists of two vertical I-bars connected by crossing curves forming an X. The left I represents Human Intelligence, the right I represents Artificial Intelligence, and the X (XR) at the center connects the two — visually expressing our mission of "Connecting Intelligences beyond Boundaries."
