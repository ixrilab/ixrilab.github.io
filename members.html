Director

Dr. Dooyoung Kim

Lecturer, Department of Computer Science & IT, La Trobe University

Dr. Dooyoung Kim is the founding director of IXRI Lab. He holds a PhD in Culture Technology (AR/VR) from KAIST, South Korea. His research focuses on the intersection of XR, AI, and human-computer interaction, with a particular emphasis on how immersive technologies can augment human capabilities.

He is a 3-time Best Paper Award winner at IEEE ISMAR, has authored 25+ publications, and has served as Co-PI on $9M+ in research grants. He pioneered the XRMemory research area and is a Global Shaper at the World Economic Forum.
