Our research areas and driving questions

2026 Research Question

"How can XR interfaces augment human capabilities through seamless Human-AI collaboration?"

Our fundamental stance: AI is not a replacement for humans, but a partner that augments human capabilities. Technology widens the radius of what humans can achieve.

Research Areas

XR

AR, VR, MR and beyond — we design and build experiential spaces that bridge reality and virtuality, enabling new forms of perception, communication, and collaboration.

AI (Artificial Intelligence)

From intelligent agents that collaborate with humans in XR to Physical AI systems that operate in the real world.

Interface & Interaction Design

Novel interfaces and interaction paradigms that make Human-AI-XR connection feel natural and seamless.
