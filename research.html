Virtual-Physical Integration

Intelligent Interface with AI

We give AI a "body" in physical space through AR head-mounted displays, enabling egocentric perception and intuitive interaction between humans and intelligent agents. Rather than confining AI to a screen, our approach embeds it directly into the user's spatial environment — allowing natural, context-aware collaboration in real-time.

XRMemory for AI Living Lab

Going beyond LLMs, we pursue real-time world understanding by collecting fragmented spatial data through standardised pipelines. XRMemory transforms everyday environments into AI living labs where spatial experiences are captured, reconstructed, and replayed — building the foundation for AI systems that truly comprehend the physical world.

Digital Twin & Physical AI

Campus-Scale Embodied Intelligence

Physical AI @ Bundoora Campus

In partnership with Cisco and NVIDIA, we are building a high-fidelity digital twin of the La Trobe University Bundoora campus inside NVIDIA Omniverse. Leveraging world models and Physical AI, we train embodied agents that perceive and interact with the real world through simulation-to-reality transfer.

Smart City Realisation with AR & Robots

By visualising invisible campus data — IoT sensors, building services, energy flows — through AR overlays and autonomous robots, we transform the campus into a living smart-city testbed. The result is an integrated ecosystem where digital twins, AR interfaces, and robotic agents work together to augment how people navigate and experience physical spaces.
