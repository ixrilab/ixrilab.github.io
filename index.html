Current Research Projects

XRMemory

XR + AI

The Future of Memory Capture, Reconstruction, and Replay in XR

How can we capture spatial experiences and replay them as if we were truly back in those moments? XRMemory investigates how XR and AI can record, reconstruct, and replay personal experiences in immersive spaces.

Springer Collection IEEE VR 2026 Workshop

Campus-Scale Embodied Intelligence

In partnership with Cisco and NVIDIA, building a high-fidelity digital twin of the La Trobe University campus in NVIDIA Omniverse — where Physical AI agents learn to perceive and interact with the world.

Research Areas

AI (Artificial Intelligence)

2026 Research Question

"How can XR interfaces augment human capabilities through seamless Human-AI collaboration?"

Our fundamental stance: AI is not a replacement for humans, but a partner that augments human capabilities. Technology widens the radius of what humans can achieve.
